Past Events

Rift War 2025

Pylon Main showcased their dominance at Rift War 2025, claiming the championship title with a 3-1 victory over Shadow Reapers. The team’s macro gameplay and objective control were unparalleled, with Phantom leading the charge in mid lane and Wraith securing every crucial Baron. The highlight of the tournament came in the final game when Spectre’s game-winning Pentakill on Jinx sealed the championship. With this triumph, Pylon Main establishes itself as one of the top League of Legends teams in 2025.

Summoner’s Rift Showdown 2025

The Summoner’s Rift Showdown 2025 delivered an unforgettable grand final, with Pylon Guardians securing the championship title after a thrilling 3-2 series against Void Slayers. With the tournament’s meta favoring high-tempo aggression, Pylon Guardians capitalized on their explosive early-game strategy. Storm, the team’s Mid Laner, dominated the series with an unmatched Azir performance, weaving through fights and securing key Emperor’s Divides that turned the tide in their favor. Meanwhile, Revenant controlled the jungle with his Sejuani, consistently setting up plays that led to crucial Baron takes. Game 5 was the most intense, as Phantom’s Jinx popped off in the final team fight, cleaning up a Quadra Kill and leading the charge to victory. This hard-fought battle cemented Pylon Guardians as one of the strongest teams in the 2025 League of Legends circuit, proving their resilience and adaptability on the grand stage.

Baron Brawl 2025

Pylon Main dominated Baron Brawl 2025, executing flawless rotations and split-pushing strategies to claim a 3-0 sweep in the Grand Finals. Wraith’s jungle control ensured every Baron Nashor went to their team, while Void’s aggressive top lane pressure kept opponents scrambling. The series concluded with Shadow’s insane outplay on Zed, eliminating three enemy carries in a single skirmish. With this victory, Pylon Main cements itself as an unstoppable force in the competitive League of Legends scene.
