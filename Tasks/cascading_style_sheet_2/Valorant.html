Past Events

E-SPORTS MANIA 2025

Pylon Nemesis delivered an unforgettable performance at E-Sports Mania 2025, securing the championship title with a 3-1 victory over Phantom Reapers. Shadow's precise Operator shots controlled the tempo, while Spectre's aggressive duels gave the team an early lead. The most thrilling moment came in the final map on Split, where Wraith clutched a 1v3 post-plant situation, securing the final round and the championship. With this win, Pylon Nemesis continues to establish itself as a dominant force in Valorant esports.

MNL ESPORTS 2025

Pylon Nemesis dominated the MNL Esports 2025 tournament, taking down Titan Slayers in a clean 3-0 sweep. The team's synergy was on full display, with Phantom leading well-coordinated site executes and Void's defensive plays holding back every enemy push. On Ascent, Spectre landed a game-changing ace, securing an eco round that turned the momentum in Pylon Nemesis' favor. With flawless teamwork and disciplined play, the team claimed another prestigious championship title.

ESGS 2022

Pylon Nemesis made a name for itself at ESGS 2022, winning its first major Valorant championship. The team fought through a stacked bracket and faced Apex Predators in a nail-biting Grand Finals. In the final map on Haven, Wraith secured a crucial clutch with a perfectly timed Viper's Pit, shutting down the enemy's final push. The tournament marked the beginning of Pylon Nemesis' journey to becoming a powerhouse in the Valorant esports scene.
