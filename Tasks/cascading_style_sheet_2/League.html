Past Events

Rift War 2025

Pylon Main showcased their dominance at Rift War 2025, claiming the championship title with a 3-1 victory over Shadow Reapers. The team’s macro gameplay and objective control were unparalleled, with Phantom leading the charge in mid lane and Wraith securing every crucial Baron. The highlight of the tournament came in the final game when Spectre’s game-winning Pentakill on Jinx sealed the championship. With this triumph, Pylon Main establishes itself as one of the top League of Legends teams in 2025.

Summoner’s Rift Showdown 2025

The Summoner’s Rift Showdown 2025 delivered an unforgettable grand final, with Pylon Guardians securing the championship title after a thrilling 3-2 series against Void Slayers. Storm, the team’s Mid Laner, dominated with unmatched Azir performances, while Revenant controlled the jungle with Sejuani. Game 5 saw Phantom’s Jinx popping off, securing a Quadra Kill and leading to victory.

Baron Brawl 2025

Pylon Main dominated Baron Brawl 2025, executing flawless rotations and split-pushing strategies to claim a 3-0 sweep in the Grand Finals. Wraith’s jungle control ensured every Baron Nashor went to their team, while Void’s aggressive top lane pressure kept opponents scrambling. The series concluded with Shadow’s insane outplay on Zed, eliminating three enemy carries in a single skirmish.
