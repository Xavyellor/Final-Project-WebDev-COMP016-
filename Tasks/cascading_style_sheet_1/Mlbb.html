The Aether Cup 2025

Pylon Nemesis showcased their dominance in the Aether Cup 2025, conquering the competition with a flawless 4-0 sweep in the Grand Finals against the Eclipse Reapers. With Shadow leading the charge in the Gold Lane and Wraith's impeccable jungle control, the team executed perfect rotations and objective control, leaving no room for their opponents to recover. The Celestial Clash 2025

Pylon Nemesis proved their dominance once again by claiming the Celestial Clash 2025 championship with a commanding 4-1 victory over the formidable Titan Slayers. Showcasing flawless synergy and aggressive map control, the team outmaneuvered their opponents in every match. Void's EXP lane pressure and Spectre's precision mid-lane plays kept the enemy on edge, while Wraith's clutch Retribution steals secured key Lords that turned the tide of battle. In the final game, Shadow delivered a Savage kill, sealing the win and adding another trophy to Pylon Nemesis' growing legacy. With this victory, the team solidifies its status as a top contender in the MLBB pro scene.
